Click on the following link: Teachable Machine. This will take you to a website. On the website, perform the instructions shown below. 

  1. Click the “Get Started” button.
  2. Select the “Image Project” option.
  3. Select “Standard Image Model”.
  4. Rename “Class 1” with "None". This represents the pictures you took without your pet.
  5. Rename "Class 2" with "[Insert Pet's Name From the Table Above]". (If you have more than one pet, continue this for each pet with Class 3, Class 4, etc.).
  6. For each class that you just renamed: Click on “Upload”. Drag and drop the corresponding folders that you downloaded from the previous page where you took photos..
  7. Once you are done uploading all the previously taken pictures. Click on train model.
  8. Once the training is complete, click on export model. 
  9. Click on "Tensorflow Lite". Select the "Floating point" option and click on "Download my model." 
  10. This will give you two files. One labeled "labels.txt" and the other "model_unquant.tflite". Chose those files and click "Submit".
